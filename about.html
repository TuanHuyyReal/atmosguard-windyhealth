Take Care Of Your Atmosphere

Air pollution is the presence of harmful substances in the air from sources like vehicles, industry, and fires. It causes serious health issues and affects nearly everyone worldwide, especially in low- and middle-income countries. Since many pollutants also contribute to climate change, reducing air pollution benefits both public health and the planet.

  • Realtime AQI Monitoring

    AtmosGuard provides up-to-the-minute data on air quality across U.S. cities. Users can see the current Air Quality Index (AQI) and understand whether the air is safe to breathe, with pollutants like PM2.5 clearly identified.

  • 5-Day Weather Forecast

    The app offers a forecast of weather for the next five days, helping users anticipate changes and plan outdoor activities accordingly. This feature is especially useful for sensitive groups like children, the elderly, or those with respiratory conditions.

  • City-Based Location Search

    Users can search for air quality data by entering the name of a U.S. city. This makes it convenient to check conditions in different areas, whether you're traveling, commuting, or simply curious.

  • Use Of A.I.

    The intergrated chatbot allows users to learn more about air quality index and the impact of a polluted atmosphere
